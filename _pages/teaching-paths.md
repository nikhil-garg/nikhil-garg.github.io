Short study routes.

<h2>BCI and Biosignals</h2>
<p>Start with EEGNet, then use EEG/ECoG, fNIRS, and EMG repositories for datasets and preprocessing.</p>

<h2>Neuromorphic Algorithms</h2>
<p>Begin with spiking-neural-network tools, then compare local learning, reservoirs, and event-driven encodings.</p>

<h2>Electronics and Open Hardware</h2>
<p>Use analog/CMOS references with FPGA, PCB, ASIC, and EDA repositories.</p>

<h2>Devices to Systems</h2>
<p>Connect RRAM, ferroelectric hafnia, OECTs, PCM, and hardware-aware simulation to system-level tasks.</p>
