Selected research and engineering projects across neuromorphic hardware, brain-computer interfaces, embedded systems, and bio-signal processing. Related papers are grouped on the [publications page](/publications/).

## UNICO

**Unsupervised spiking neural networks with analog memristive devices for edge computing.** Developed voltage-dependent synaptic plasticity rules, memristive synapse models, simulation workflows, and mixed-signal ASIC prototypes for local learning in spiking neural networks.

[Project page](https://www.chistera.eu/projects/unico) · [VDSP_Nengo](https://github.com/nikhil-garg/VDSP_Nengo) · [VDSP-Memristors](https://github.com/nikhil-garg/VDSP-Memristors) · [selected papers](/publications/#unico--in-memory-learning)

## IONOS

**Iono-electronic neuromorphic interfaces for communication with living systems.** Built analog-to-spike conversion workflows, EMG reservoir-computing experiments, and organic electrochemical transistor reservoir evaluation frameworks.

[Project page](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/773228) · [EMG_exp](https://github.com/nikhil-garg/EMG_exp) · [selected papers](/publications/#ionos--bio-signal-processing)

## Brain-Computer Interfaces

Real-time EEG and machine-learning pipelines for assistive technology, motor imagery, biometric authentication, cognitive-state estimation, and portable-headset emotion decoding.

[Decoding_EEG](https://github.com/nikhil-garg/Decoding_EEG) · [selected papers](/publications/#bci--eeg)

## FPGA and Embedded Hardware

FPGA, PCB, and hardware-software co-design work for memory characterization, embedded acceleration, ADC/DAC interfacing, and signal-processing systems.

[CV](/cv/) · [selected papers](/publications/#analog-ai-hardware)

## VITFOX

Ferroelectric-oxide hardware and algorithm work for neural-network training, including energy-convergence tradeoffs and bio-inspired learning strategies on emerging devices.

[EEG-Ferro](https://github.com/NEO-ETHZ/EEG-Ferro) · [Energy-convergence-Ferro-CCMCC2026](https://github.com/nikhil-garg/Energy-convergence-Ferro-CCMCC2026) · [selected papers](/publications/#vitfox--ferroelectric-learning)

## SWIMS

Current postdoctoral work in the Bio-Inspired Circuits and Systems group at the University of Groningen, in the scope of the ERC project SWIMS: Stochastic Spiking Wireless Multimodal Sensory System.

[Bio-Inspired Circuits and Systems group](https://www.rug.nl/research/zernike/bio-inspired-circuits-and-systems/)
