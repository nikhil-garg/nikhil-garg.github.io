In-Memory Computing SoC with Multi-level RRAM to Accelerate AI Inference

Yin Wenbo•2025

Abstract: TetraMem will introduce its multi-level RRAM (Resistive Random-Access Memory) cell for in-memory computing. The talk will explain how TetraMem uses Multi-level RRAM to accelerate neural network inference applications. The speaker will demonstrate how TetraMem leverages its unique technology and expertise to increase precision, accuracy and energy efficiency of AI applications, including our methods to improve cell performance as recently published in Nature and Science. Bio Wenbo Yin is the SVP of IC Design at TetraMem.
