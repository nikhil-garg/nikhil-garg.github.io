CONSOLIDATING TINYML LIFECYCLE WITH LARGE LANGUAGE MODELS with ROBERTO MORABITO of EURECOM Unlock the future of technology with insights from AI industry pioneers as we journey into the transformative realm of generative AI at the edge. This day of talks promises to unravel the complexities of how Large Language Models (LLMs) and transformer models are set to accelerate edge computing, reshaping both the TinyML landscape and our everyday experiences. As we move beyond the realm of chatbots, our impressive roster of speakers - including people from Meta, BOSCH, STMicroelectronics, Kyung Hee University, FBK, and Wipro - provide a deep dive into the latest advancements in algorithms, software, chips, and tools that are democratizing generative AI for the masses. Join us in a vibrant discussion fueled by the collective energy and curiosity of our community, powered by the unwavering support of the TinyML Foundation and strategic partners. Discover the groundbreaking transformations that lie ahead and be inspired by the exchange of ideas that could spark further innovation. The groundwork laid by previous forums has set the stage for this thrilling exploration, where we aim to inspire and engage all listeners, highlighting the monumental impact that generative AI at the edge will have on technology and daily life. Don’t miss out on this opportunity to hear from the trailblazers shaping our future.
