Neuromorphic computing: from theory to applications, Lecture 1 – ACACES 2023

Sandamirskaya Yulia•HiPEAC•2023•186 views

Join Yulia Sandamirskaya, head of the Cognitive Computing in Life Sciences research centre at Zurich University of Applied Sciences (ZHAW) and senior researcher in Intel's Neuromorphic Computing Lab, for a journey into the theory behind neuromorphic computing.
