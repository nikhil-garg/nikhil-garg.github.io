D’Angelo Giulia•2024

The VCMI Invited Talks #5 counted with the presentation of Giulia D'Angelo. Title of the presentation: What's catching your eye? Bioinspired and neuromorphic algorithms to model visual attention Description of the presentation: Giulia is a Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions (MSCA) Postdoctoral Fellow at the Czech Technical University (https://www.cvut.cz/en) in Prague. In this talk, she presented her work on neuromorphic algorithms to model visual attention.
