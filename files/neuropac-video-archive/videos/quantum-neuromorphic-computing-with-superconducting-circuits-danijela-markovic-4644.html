Quantum neuromorphic computing with superconducting circuits - Danijela Marković

Markovic Danijela•2024

Danijela Marković, a permanent CNRS researcher at the CNRS/Thales laboratory, gives an invited talk on "Quantum neuromorphic computing with superconducting circuits" at the conference on computing with physical systems in January 2024. Danijela's research website can be found here: https://www.neurophysics.cnrs-thales.fr/people/danijela-markovic/ The conference for computing with physical systems website can be found here: https://mcmahon.aep.cornell.edu/aspen/2024/
